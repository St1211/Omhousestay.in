About Our Homestay

Discover the authentic beauty of Ladakh's Moonland with our warm hospitality

Your Himalayan Sanctuary in Moonland

Nestled in the heart of Lamayuru, famously known as the "Moonland" for its unique lunar landscapes and ancient monastery, OM Homestay offers a perfect blend of traditional Ladakhi culture and authentic comfort.

Our homestay is family-run, ensuring personalized attention and genuine experiences. Wake up to breathtaking views of the Himalayan mountains and the mystical moon-like terrain, savor traditional Ladakhi cuisine, and immerse yourself in the tranquility of this spiritual land.

Whether you're a trekker seeking adventure or a traveler looking for peace, our homestay provides the perfect base to explore the wonders of Ladakh's Moonland region.

Lamayuru Monastery

One of the oldest and largest monasteries in Ladakh, founded in the 11th century. Known as "Yungdrung" (the swastika), it's a spiritual center with stunning traditional architecture overlooking the Moonland landscape.

5 minutes walk

Moonland Landscape

Breathtaking views of the unique lunar landscape that gives Lamayuru its nickname "Moonland." Perfect for photography, meditation, and experiencing the surreal beauty of eroded mountains resembling the moon's surface.

Right from our homestay

Getting Here

By Road

We are located 107km west of Leh on the Srinagar-Leh Highway (NH1). The journey takes approximately 2-3 hours by car. Look for our traditional sign just before Lamayuru village.

By Bus

Regular buses run from Leh to Lamayuru. The bus stop is 200m from our homestay. We can arrange pickup from the bus stop with advance notice.

By Air

The nearest airport is Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport in Leh (IXL), approximately 107km away. We can arrange traditional transport with advance booking.
