About OM Homestay

Your Himalayan Sanctuary

Nestled in the mystical Moonland of Lamayuru, OM Homestay offers traditional Ladakhi hospitality with modern comfort. Wake up to surreal lunar landscapes, enjoy organic local meals, and explore the ancient Lamayuru Monastery just a walk away.
