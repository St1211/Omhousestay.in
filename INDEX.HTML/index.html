About Our Homestay

Discover the authentic beauty of Ladakh with our warm hospitality

Your Himalayan Sanctuary

Nestled in the heart of Lamayuru, known for its ancient monastery and moon-like landscapes, OM Homestay offers a perfect blend of traditional Ladakhi culture and modern comfort.

Our homestay is family-run, ensuring personalized attention and authentic experiences. Wake up to breathtaking views of the Himalayas, savor traditional Ladakhi cuisine, and immerse yourself in the tranquility of this spiritual land.

Whether you're a trekker seeking adventure or a traveler looking for peace, our homestay provides the perfect base to explore the wonders of Ladakh.

Getting Here

By Road

We are located 107km west of Leh on the Srinagar-Leh Highway (NH1). The journey takes approximately 2-3 hours by car. Look for our sign just before Lamayuru village.

By Bus

Regular buses run from Leh to Lamayuru. The bus stop is 200m from our homestay. We can arrange pickup from the bus stop with advance notice.

By Air

The nearest airport is Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport in Leh (IXL), approximately 107km away. We can arrange airport transfers with advance booking.
